Ada Lovelace

Mega awesome computer programmer before the computer was invented!

By my understanding, there was a mathematician named Charles Babbage, who created a mechanical polynomial calculator. He then created a design for a more complicated mechanical computer, which was never built. This "analytical engine" is considered the first design for a turing complete computer.

Ada Lovelace was a member of English aristocracy who was familiar to Charles Babbage, and was the first to realize that Babbage's analytical engine could be used for more than mere calculation. She wrote the first ever computer program, to be executed on the analytical engine.
